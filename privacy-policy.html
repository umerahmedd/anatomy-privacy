Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Human Anatomy ("the App") is an educational 3D anatomy viewer. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All interactions happen locally on your device.

  • No account or sign-up required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers

3. Camera Access

The App may request camera access solely for the Augmented Reality (AR) feature. Camera data is processed on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or shared.

4. Photos & Media

Screenshots captured within the App are saved directly to your device's photo library. We do not access, upload, or share these files.

5. Third-Party Services

The App loads the Three.js library from a CDN to render 3D models. No personal data is sent to third-party servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is educational in nature and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
